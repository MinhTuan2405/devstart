## Ghi chu

- `{slug}` la slug cua tung bai hoc, bai interview hoac bai blog.
- Cac trang con nhieu noi dung chi duoc ghi tuong trung, khong liet ke tung bai cu the.
- Header hien co gom: Trang chu, Khoa hoc, Interview, Blog, Gioi thieu.
