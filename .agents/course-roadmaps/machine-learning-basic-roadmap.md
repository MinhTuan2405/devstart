# Roadmap khoa hoc Machine Learning co ban

## Muc tieu

- Xay dung khoa hoc Machine Learning co ban bang tieng Viet cho nguoi moi bat dau
- Moi bai hoc nham vao mot y dinh tim kiem ro rang de de SEO
- Lo trinh uu tien nen tang, thuc hanh, va cac mo hinh kinh dien truoc khi mo rong sang chu de nang cao

## Nguyen tac SEO

- Moi bai hoc chi tap trung mot topic chinh va mot main keyword chinh
- Uu tien cum tu khoa beginner intent: `la gi`, `cho nguoi moi`, `huong dan`, `vi du`, `cach dung`
- Dat bai tong quan truoc, bai ky thuat sau de tao internal linking tu nhom khai niem sang nhom mo hinh
- Giu slug va tieu de sat voi cach nguoi hoc Viet Nam tim kiem
